About australian armed forces

Australian Armed Forces

The #1 Australian ARMA 3 Community

The Australian Armed Forces, often referred to as just “AAF”, is a co-op based squad formed in late 2008, however its roots extend back to 2001 with the release of Operation Flashpoint.

Structured and Organised

Major Missions on every Thursday and Sunday

We play nearly every evening with at least one major game every week and have members on our TS3 almost every day. We have scheduled mission nights and often organize/participate in joint-operations with other community squads.
